Bottom-bar refactor

Three surgical changes per @crutkas: (1) the bottom-right “IsoToUsb · MIT” caption is removed, (2) the status pill on the left gains a small shield glyph prefix only while IsBusy is true, and (3) a tiny tertiary caption — “Work done with elevated worker process” — sits below the existing Setup-card footer badge.

Net result: the bottom bar collapses from two rows to one, and elevation context moves into the pill (during work) and the Setup card (always-on attribution).
